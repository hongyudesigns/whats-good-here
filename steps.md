- 2026-03-14 — Breathing gradient animation (complete rethink): Replaced background-image gradients with a fixed full-viewport `.gradient-layer` behind the shell, rendering three large blurred blue blobs plus a permanent white-peak blob at the top center. In `page.tsx`, the layer is always present; when `status === "loading"` it receives `gradient-layer--loading`, which drives smooth `transform` + `opacity` animations on each blue blob via `blobDriftSlow`, `blobDriftMedium`, and `blobDriftFast` keyframes. After the latest tune, blobs now drift ~20–25% with opacity between 0.2 and 0.9 at 4s/3s/2s durations (Aurora-level motion), and the white peak is a 400px, opacity-1.0 blob at the top center so it’s clearly visible.
- 2026-03-14 — Previous search pill entrance animation: The newest history pill (index 0) now animates in with a dot→pill→label sequence. Added `.pill-enter` and `.pill-enter__label` classes: the pill scales from 0.1→1.1→1.0 on the X-axis with a springy cubic-bezier, and the label fades in after a short delay. Wired in `page.tsx` by applying `pill-enter` / `pill-enter__label` to the first pill when history is rendered.
